Tom

"Building Connections"

Routine is something that scares both Tom and I alike. Dealing with that fear is something I'm still working on, but it seems like Tom has it figured out.

He managed to combine a love of sport and a desire to travel with the need for a variable work day into a career in sports broadcasting.

Tackling big projects like bringing professional football (read: soccer) teams to Iran and working on the World Cup in Brazil brings out a lot of crazy stories, but my big takeaway was that Tom made lasting connections in the places that he worked.

Case in point: Tom's entire Budapest trip was centred around meeting friends he had made in Brazil. He took advantage of his time working abroad to build relationships with the locals, and he's begun to build a network across the globe. Before too long Tom will have a couch to crash on and a friend to grab a beer with in every major city.

Hearing about Tom's friendships makes me all the more determined to immerse myself in Danish culture while I'm here. Hopefully, like Tom, I'll make more than a few true friends during my time in Copenhagen.
