Rui

The Upside Down Girl

When people ask me how I met Rui I always laugh a little. If she's in the room I'll give her a smile, and ask her to tell the story. It's always fun trying to guess what version of the story she'll tell.

Sometimes she says that we met in Rome, through our mutual friend Jack. (LINK THIS LATER)

After some brief small talk (I was flirting with one of her friends), I found out that Rachel was from Toronto. I was excited to have a new Canadian friend. So excited that I insisted that her and her friends join me in a team cheer for Canada. I led the countdown, threw my hand in the air when it was over, and then promptly left, never to be seen again.

Maybe my subconscious realized how cringe inducing my actions were and decided that it was time for me to take a lap. I disappeared for the night.

Either way, it was a long time before I saw Rachel or her friends again. To them I was probably "that weird Canadian dude from Tietgen", but I'd like to think that I shook that reputation when I met them properly.

I got to know Rachel through a happy coincidence(link to Rui), and I'm glad that I did. She's a killer story teller, has a quirky sense of humour, and wants to start a podcast. Oh and she snorts when she laughs. For some reason I feel like that's key to understanding her character.

Even though we didn't have the most conventional start to a friendship, I'm glad that we eventually ended up getting to know each other. If you're reading this Rachel, when you start that podcast, let me know if you tell the story of that wack Canadian from Copenhagen. I'd love to listen.
